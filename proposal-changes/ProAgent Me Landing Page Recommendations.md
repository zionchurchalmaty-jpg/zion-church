# ProAgent Me Landing Page Recommendations

> Proposed updates to https://www.proagentme.com/ to support the dual-vertical strategy (Developers + Legal/Accounting)

---

## Executive Summary

The current landing page positions ProAgent Me well for creators (experts), but the dual-vertical strategy requires updates to:

1. **Add client-focused messaging** emphasizing the "trust gap" for professional services
2. **Showcase multiple verticals** (Developers, Tax/Accounting, Legal)
3. **Highlight human escalation** as a key differentiator for professional services
4. **Add vertical-specific signup paths** to track demand by industry

---

## Current State Analysis

**What's working:**

- Clean design
- Clear creator value proposition
- Privacy promise
- Waitlist capture

**What needs updating:**

- Limited client-facing messaging (mostly creator-focused)
- Single vertical implied (developers/tech)
- Human escalation not prominently featured
- No vertical-specific paths for tracking demand
- "Trust gap" opportunity not addressed
